Why super matters here

As a sole trader, voluntary super contributions are fully tax-deductible when you lodge a Notice of Intent with your fund.

This reduces taxable income — coming off the top of your highest bracket. At $96k gross income, that's saving 32.5c in the dollar.

The 11.5% rate is the 2025–26 employer baseline. You can contribute more (up to $30k concessionally) to reduce tax further.

Always confirm with your accountant.
